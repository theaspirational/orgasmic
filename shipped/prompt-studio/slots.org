** Substitution
- Syntax: {{namespace.field}}
- Strict variable substitution. No conditionals, no loops, no expressions.
- Evaluated only inside ** Spawn-time injection and ** Continuation injection
  body sections of a prompt template.
- Unknown slot at compile time = compile error; surfaced in dry-run.

** Available slots

*** task.*
- task.id, task.title, task.description, task.priority, task.tags
- task.acceptance        (** Acceptance Criteria section content)
- task.write_scope, task.read_scope
- task.test_cmd          (per-task TEST_CMD; resolves from the heading, else the config default — TASK-Q68WE)
- task.depends_on
- task.activity          (materialized task activity feed: comments, state transitions, run lifecycle)

*** dispatch.*
- dispatch.brief         (manager handoff content supplied with a dispatch; non-dispatch compiles use "not set")

*** project.*
- project.id, project.name, project.path
- project.test_cmd, project.lint_cmd, project.build_cmd
- project.default_branch

*** worker.*
- worker.id, worker.kind
- worker.persona            (** Persona section of the agent template)
- worker.operating_rules    (** Operating Rules section)

*** skills.*
- skills.all                (manifest of all linked skills: id, description, triggers, abs path)
- skills.<id>               (single skill body, inline)
- skill_path.<id>           (single skill's absolute path, string)

*** conventions.*
- conventions.<name>        (body of shipped/conventions/<name>.org, or user/conventions/<name>.org when overridden; resolved via the loader rule per dec_GRDFB)

*** run.*
- run.id, run.iteration_count, run.previous_state

*** evidence.*
- evidence.so_far           (** Evidence section of the claimed task)

*** worklog.*
- worklog.tail              (last N worklog entries from the claimed task)

*** artifact.*
- artifact.subject_nodes    (subject nodes' Org content plus their graph neighborhood; not task-scoped, so no ={{task.*}}= slots are used by the artifact-generator spec)
- artifact.user_prompt      (the short prompt from the Generate dialog, appended on every run including regenerations)
- artifact.regen_context    (regenerate only: prior artifact.mdx, all current-version comments with author/resolution/anchor, and the optional extra prompt; "not set" on a first generate)
