#+orgasmic_version: 1

* CONVENTION architecture_decomposition
:PROPERTIES:
:NAME:       architecture_decomposition
:NAMESPACE:  conventions
:APPLIES_TO: architector
:VERSION:    1
:END:

Use this convention when the architector composes or revises architecture graph
nodes.

** principle-01 a leaf has a stable contract
** principle-02 a leaf is verb-namable in one short sentence
** principle-03 mechanism, not feature — cross-cutting infrastructure gets first-class leaves
** principle-04 source path budget 1–10 per leaf
** principle-05 children per parent 2–6
** principle-06 no mechanism-as-mechanism leaves ("all hooks", "all handlers")
** principle-07 no cross-cutting-concern leaves (logging, error handling)
** principle-08 tests, build scripts, fixtures excluded from =:SOURCE_PATHS:=
** principle-09 bootstrap from existing module boundaries first
** principle-10 target/planned nodes stay separate visually and are excluded from drift enforcement
